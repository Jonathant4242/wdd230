Target Audience

This site is target business and residence to the Rapel. The goal is to show a fast past, developing town. Highlighting the city's attractions and world class education. Go over some development in process.

Personas

Persona #1: A family looking for competive education for their children. With great opertunities to own their own business.

Persona #2: Someone looking to invest in the tourist industry. We want to deminstrate we have a streamline approval process for developers.

Scenarios

Why would people go visit a city of commerce site.

  • Digital marketing for local business.
  • Residence, checking in to see if there are city approved events.
  • Tourist, looking for what attractions or events happening.
  • Business, checking the status of permits or upcomming developments.
  • Future residence and investors, looking for what the city has to offer.
